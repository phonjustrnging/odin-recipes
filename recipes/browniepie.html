Browniepie Recipe

This brownie pie is rich and fudgy with a perfectly crisp crust. Definitely serve with ice cream, whipped cream, and salted caramel.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, plus more for rolling
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ cup cold unsalted butter, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 3 tablespoons ice water, or more as needed

Steps

  1. 10 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  2. ¾ cup granulated sugar
  3. 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  4. 1 large egg yolk, at room temperature
  5. 2 tablespoons canola oil
  6. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  8. ½ cup all-purpose flour
  9. 2 teaspoons instant espresso granules
  10. ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  11. ½ cup chopped walnuts or hazelnuts
  12. ½ cup semisweet chocolate chips, divided
